Venison Barbecue

Descritpion

Venison steaks, barbecued in italian dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 4 (1/2 pound) venison steaks

Steps

  1. Marinate the venison meat over night in the Italian dressing. Flip over at least one time during the marinade.
  2. Cook meat on the grill over medium-high heat. Cook for 20 to 30 minutes or until done to your taste.
